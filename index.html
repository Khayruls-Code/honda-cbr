Frequently Asked Questions

Verification of recall status can be found using our Recall Information Page. Please have your VIN (Vehicle Identification Number) available. If your recall hasn’t been completed, please contact an authorized Honda Powersports dealer for more information.

American Honda Powersports Customer Relations does not offer technical assistance. American Honda Powersports division relies on our authorized Honda Powersports dealers to be your technical resource. Please utilize the ‘Find a Dealer’ feature located on the top right of our webpage to find an authorized Honda Powersports dealer nearest you.

You can order replacement ignition keys through your local authorized Honda Powersports dealer. The dealer will need your key code to cut a new ignition key. If you do not have the key code, we first recommend contacting your original selling dealer since they may have recorded this information at the time of sale. American Honda Powersports division is unable to provide a key code that corresponds with your VIN. If you are still unable to find your key code, please contact your local authorized Honda Powersports dealer for further assistance in obtaining a replacement key.

All U.S. Honda Powersports products have a 17-digit alphanumeric code that is stamped on the frame. Also, you can find your VIN on your dealer sales agreement documents, insurance identification card, or your state’s registration documents.
